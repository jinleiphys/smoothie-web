SMOOTHIE

Scattering Model of Optical Operator Theory for Ichimura-Austern-Vincent Equations

A sophisticated nuclear reaction code developed by Jin Lei and Antonio M. Moro for performing non-elastic breakup calculations in inclusive breakup reactions using the formalism of Ichimura, Austern and Vincent. SMOOTHIE is a modern Fortran program for nuclear physics simulations and scattering calculations in breakup reactions.
