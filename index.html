SMOOTHIE

Scattering Model of Optical Operator Theory for Ichimura-Austern-Vincent Equations

A sophisticated computer code developed by Jin Lei and Antonio M. Moro for performing non-elastic breakup calculations in inclusive breakup reactions using the formalism of Ichimura, Austern and Vincent.
